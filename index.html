The Final Frontier.

Isolated and immense, there is no place like this and no further place than this. A boundless white canvas of ice and snow, Antarctica is instantly alluring, and its quiet solitude moves you in a way like no other place does, and no other can. Barren and hostile, it is the stage for one of the world’s most perilous migrations, that of the emperor penguin. Witnessing a part of this epic phenomenon is unforgettable and offers a rare glimpse into the cycle of life. The last continent to be discovered, even now it still feels like you’re one of the first explorers. This is an unforgiving land of endless canyons and towering mountains, but if you’re among the precious few that visit every year, you will undoubtedly come away seeing the world through different eyes. Its scale is almost incomprehensible, the whole experience is undeniably emotional, and that’s exactly why you should go.
